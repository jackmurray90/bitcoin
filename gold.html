As of July 2023, it's estimated that the entire value of all the gold ever mined is around $12.25 trillion. In contrast, the overall market cap for bitcoin is roughly $850 billion.
Bitcoin has all the desirable properties of gold that make it suitable for money, plus some even more desirable properties such as the ability to transfer it instantly internationally and total scarcity. I personally believe a fair valuation would see Bitcoin valued closer to gold, if not more than gold.
